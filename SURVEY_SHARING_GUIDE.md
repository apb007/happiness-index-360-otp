# How to Share Happiness Index 360° Surveys

## Step 1: Generate a Public Survey Link

As a School Administrator:

1. Go to **Dashboard → Reports → Public Survey**
2. Click **"Create New Survey Link"**
3. Set expiration date (optional)
4. Click **"Generate Token"**
5. Copy the generated link

## Step 2: Share the Link with Students

Your survey link will look like:
```
https://[your-domain].com/survey/[unique-token]
```

Share this link via:
- **Email** to class distribution list
- **Learning management system** (Google Classroom, Canvas, etc.)
- **QR code** (generate at [qrcode.com](https://qrcode.com))
- **School website** or portal
- **SMS** or messaging app (if permitted)

## Step 3: Students Complete the Survey

When students click the link:
1. They see a clean, mobile-friendly assessment form
2. No login required (fully anonymous)
3. Takes about 10 minutes to complete
4. Responses are saved securely
5. Automatic thank you page with crisis resources

## Step 4: Collect Results

All responses are automatically:
- Stored securely in your database
- Tracked with timestamps
- Analyzed for risk levels
- Included in your reports

## Access Results

### As an Administrator:
1. Go to **Dashboard → Reports**
2. View school-wide analytics:
   - Average Happiness Index score
   - Risk distribution (pie chart)
   - Total assessments completed
3. Click **"Download CSV Report"** to get raw data

### As a Teacher:
1. Go to **Dashboard → Classes → [Your Class]**
2. See class average Happiness Index
3. View individual student results (if in your class)
4. Identify students needing support

### What You'll See:

**School Average Happiness Index: 62.5/100**
- Critical Risk: 5 students
- High Risk: 12 students
- Moderate Risk: 28 students
- Low Risk: 55 students

## Data Privacy Assurance

✓ All responses are **completely anonymous** (no names collected)
✓ Students **cannot be identified** from survey responses
✓ Results are **encrypted** and stored securely
✓ Only **school administrators** can see data
✓ Data can be **deleted** upon request
✓ **GDPR/COPPA compliant** for all ages

---

## Tips for High Response Rates

1. **Timing**: Send link during class time for higher completion
2. **Incentives**: Offer small incentives (extra credit, raffle entry)
3. **Reminders**: Send 1-2 reminder emails after initial send
4. **Mobile-friendly**: Use QR code for classroom completion
5. **Teacher support**: Have teachers explain importance of honest responses

---

## Technical Support

**Survey link not working?**
- Verify token hasn't expired
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Try different device/browser

**Can't download results?**
- Ensure you have admin access
- Check internet connection
- Try downloading at different time
- Contact support if issue persists

**Questions about data?**
- See DATA_SECURITY.md for full compliance details
- Contact your school's data protection officer
- File data request with administrator
