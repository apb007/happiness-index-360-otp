# Getting Started with Happiness Index 360°

## For Administrators

### 1. Create Your School Account
- Sign up at `https://[your-domain].com/auth/sign-up`
- Select "Principal/Administrator" role
- Create your school profile
- Confirm your email

### 2. Create Classes
- Go to **Dashboard → Classes**
- Click **"+ New Class"**
- Add class name, grade level, teacher
- Assign students to class

### 3. Share Survey Links with Students
**Option A: Anonymous Survey (Recommended for First Use)**
- Go to **Dashboard → Principal → Public Survey Links**
- Click **"+ New Survey Link"**
- Copy the link and share with students via:
  - Email classroom distribution list
  - Learning management system (Google Classroom, Canvas)
  - QR code (generate at qrcode.com)
  - School announcement

**Option B: Direct Student Logins**
- Create student accounts via **Dashboard → Users → Add Student**
- Share login credentials with students
- Students take assessment from their dashboard

### 4. Monitor Results
- Go to **Dashboard → Reports**
- View school-wide Happiness Index average
- See risk distribution pie chart
- Check individual class averages
- Download CSV report for analysis

### 5. Take Action
- **For Critical/High Risk Students**:
  - Alert school counselor immediately
  - Contact parents/guardians
  - Follow your school's crisis protocol
- **For Moderate Risk Students**:
  - Schedule meeting with counselor
  - Offer support resources
  - Track progress with repeat assessments

## For Teachers

### 1. Access Your Dashboard
- Login at `https://[your-domain].com`
- You'll see your assigned classes

### 2. Monitor Your Class
- Click on a class to see all students
- View each student's latest Happiness Index score
- See risk level indicator (color-coded)
- Identify students needing support

### 3. View Student Details
- Click a student name to see:
  - Assessment history
  - Score trends (chart)
  - Risk level interpretation
- Take notes about concerns

### 4. Report Concerns
- Notify school counselor of at-risk students
- Use the "Alerts" feature to flag urgent cases
- Schedule follow-up conversations

---

## For Students

### 1. Complete Assessment
- Click the survey link from teacher/administrator
- **No login required** (fully anonymous)
- Answer 32 questions honestly
- Takes about 10 minutes
- Submit assessment

### 2. View Your Results
- See your Happiness Index score (0-100)
- Understand your risk level (critical, high, moderate, low)
- Get personalized resources and recommendations
- View crisis support contacts

### 3. Track Progress
- Retake assessment periodically (monthly recommended)
- See your score history chart
- Monitor improvement over time
- Share results with counselor if comfortable

## FAQ

**Q: Is this survey anonymous?**
A: Yes! Public survey links don't require login or names. Students can complete assessments completely anonymously.

**Q: Can students see each other's results?**
A: No. Only school administrators and teachers (for their classes) can see results.

**Q: How long does the assessment take?**
A: About 10 minutes on average.

**Q: Can I export the data?**
A: Yes! Go to **Reports → Download CSV** to export all assessment data.

**Q: What if a student scores critical risk?**
A: The assessment shows crisis resources (988, Crisis Text Line). School should follow your crisis protocol and contact parents immediately.

**Q: How often should students complete the assessment?**
A: We recommend monthly for ongoing monitoring, or quarterly minimum for tracking trends.

**Q: Can I customize the questions?**
A: Currently, the assessment uses the standard 32-question Happiness Index 360°. Custom questions coming in future updates.

## Technical Support

**Issue: Students can't access survey link**
- Verify link was copied correctly
- Check student internet connection
- Try different device/browser
- Contact support if issue persists

**Issue: Results not showing**
- Wait 1-2 minutes for data to process
- Refresh page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Check that assessments were submitted (not abandoned)

**Issue: Can't download CSV**
- Ensure you have admin access
- Try different browser
- Check internet connection
- Contact technical support
